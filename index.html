About

I'm a technical sound designer based in Prague with ten years of experience in music (jazz conservatory) and a background in software development (C#, Ruby, Python). I specialise in adaptive audio for interactive media — designing the sound, building the systems that drive it, and implementing it in the engine. Alongside audio work I teach Unity and visual programming at a secondary technical school.

Audio middleware

FMOD Studio — event design, state-based mixing, parameters, banks. Working knowledge of Wwise.

DAW & sound creation

Ableton Live (Drift synthesis, sampling, mixing, mastering), Cubase, Reaper, Pro Tools. Field recording.

Game engines

Unity 6 — URP, C# scripting, design patterns (state machine), audio integration.

Programming & music

C#, Ruby, Python. Jazz harmony, production, arrangement, and 10+ years of live performance.
